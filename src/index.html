The City Chain Foundation is a non-profit organization that develops and maintains City Chain and the Smart City Platform. The foundation also works to spread information about City Coin, cryptocoins and blockchains.

The foundation manages funds that are used to fund and support projects directly, or indirectly related to City Chain and the Smart City Platform.

Opportunities

Community Manager

We are looking for a Community Manager, which will initially be a part-time job helping growing the community around City Chain and the Smart City Platform.

As we are in the early days of City Chain, you will be able to help set the foundation for how we interact with the community, and will later be able to onboard new crew for the community efforts and manage your own budget.

The job is primarily based on remote working, and payments will be based on City Coin (CITY). City Chain Foundation is building offices in Liberstad in 2019, and are planning on opening offices in Liberland, when possible.

If this is interesting to you, please forward you CV and application to our e-mail address at post@city-chain.org.

Funding

City Chain Foundation received a donation of City Coin from the initial launch of the blockchain. These funds are planned to be used to ensure the continued development, operations and promotion, of City Chain and the Smart City Platform.
The donations are given to support the foundation for 200 years.


Majority of the funds will go to continued development for many years, community building and marketing.

Funded projects

Applying for funding to a project can be submitted on this website in the future. The foundation will consider all applications of suggested projects and might support projects with funding.

Funding will start after general availability of the City Chain blockchain.
