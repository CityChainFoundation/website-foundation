The City Chain Foundation is a non-profit organization that works to spread information about City Coin, cryptocoins and blockchains.

The foundation manages funds that are used to fund and support projects directly, or indirectly related to City Chain and the Smart City Platform.

The foundation can and will fund projects of many kinds, not just software-based projects. Implementation and usage of City Chain and the Smart City Platform by individuals, organizations, cities and municipalities might receive funding. Innovative, new and modern cities can also apply for funding.

Applying for funding to a project can be submitted on this website in the future.
